PhD thesis

In December 2018, I defended my PhD thesis on the Czech philosopher and human rights activist, Jan Patočka. The title of my thesis was A Ship Bound to Shipwreck. Jan Patočka's Philosophy of History. I am currently preparing the manuscript for eventual publication as a book.
