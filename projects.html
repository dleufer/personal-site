Projects

Ongoing Projects

Access Now: From March to July 2019, I am working on a research internship on AI policy at Access Now, in particular focusing on the EU's Ethics Guidelines for Trustworthy AI.

Open Philosophy Network: In January 2019, I set up the Open Philosophy Network to provide a platform for philosophy outside of academia, with a particular focus on philosophy of technology and tech ethics.

We currently organise a monthly reading group and have been going to tech events and hackathons to open the conversation on tech ethics

Working Group on Philosophy of Technology (WGPT): I am also a member of the Working Group on Philosophy of Technology at KU Leuven. We are a group of philosophers linked to the Institute of Philosophy (HIW) whose aim is to philosophically reflect on technology, in a way that is informed by scientific research, technological developments, and the history of philosophy of technology. We organize evening lectures, a reading group and will organise an international conference on technology and society in September 2019.
